Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Overview

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data KudiSave ("the App") collects, how we use it, and how we protect it.

KudiSave is owned and operated by Adusei Media.

Data We Collect

When you use KudiSave, we may collect the following information:

  • Account info — Name, email address, phone number (for SMS OTP verification), and password (encrypted)
  • Financial data — Expenses, income, budgets, goals, savings, bill reminders, and subscriptions you enter
  • App usage — Streaks, achievements, challenges completed, and your preferences
  • Device info — Browser type, screen size, and theme preference (for display purposes only)
  • Gmail data — Only if you opt-in to Gmail OAuth. We request permission to read your Gmail inbox to detect bill-related emails. We do NOT access, store, or share your emails with third parties.

We do not collect bank account numbers, mobile money PINs, payment card details, or device location.

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the KudiSave service
  • Generate personalized spending insights and reports
  • Track your financial goals and daily streaks
  • Send email notifications (if you opt in):
    • Daily bill payment reminders
    • Weekly financial summaries
    • Monthly budget alerts
  • Verify your account via SMS OTP and email
  • Allow you to export and backup your data

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security

We take security seriously and implement the following measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt (industry-standard encryption)
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens stored locally in your browser
  • All API communication is encrypted over HTTPS
  • Database access is restricted and monitored
  • Rate limiting is applied to prevent abuse and brute-force attacks
  • All authentication endpoints have strict validation and limiting

While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. You are responsible for keeping your login credentials safe and logging out on shared devices.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. Some preferences may also be stored locally on your device (localStorage) for a faster experience.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, your data is permanently removed from our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — View and export your financial data in CSV, Excel, or PDF formats
  • Correct — Update any inaccurate information in your profile or financial records
  • Delete — Permanently remove your account and all associated data
  • Opt out — Disable email notifications (bill reminders, summaries, alerts) at any time
  • Disconnect — Revoke Gmail OAuth access without deleting your account

You can exercise these rights from the Settings page or by contacting us directly. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Cookies & Local Storage

KudiSave uses localStorage to save the following on your device:

  • JWT authentication token — Keeps you logged in between sessions
  • Theme preference — Your light/dark mode choice
  • User preferences — Language, currency, notification settings

We do not use third-party tracking cookies, analytics, or external data collection services. We load Lucide icons from a CDN for performance, but this is a static icon library with no tracking.

You can clear localStorage at any time through your browser settings, which will log you out.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.
